Duke Arts Festival Call for Submissions
Start: 00:00
End: 00:00
Location: Bryan Center
Description: CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS. We seek work by student artists to present in exhibition and performance settings during the 2011 Duke Arts Festival, which runs from October 28 through November 6, 2011. All visual art forms and performance genres accepted, including painting, photography, sculpture, film, poetry, readings, dance-individual and ensemble work, musical performances-soloists, ensemble, orchestra, vocalists, choirs, bands, performance installations, theater, happenings, and more! Performances will take place at locations on both East and West Campus; the Exhibition will be hung in the Bryan Center. The Festival kicks off on Parents' and Family Weekend and will end on DEMAN (Duke Entertainment, Media and the Arts Network) weekend with workshops and presentations by Duke Alumni who have established careers in the arts. Follow the link for instructions and forms. Submissions will be accepted until October 14. Questions? Call 919.684.0540. Follow the Duke Arts Festival on Facebook.
